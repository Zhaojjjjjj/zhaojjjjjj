In fact, the process of creating capital relations is like the process of separating workers from labor ownership, which can not only transform social means of life and means of production into capital, but also separate producers from means of production. Although this process appears' primitive ', as a historical process, it has important practical significance for the formation of capital and the corresponding production methods. It is precisely due to the role of primitive accumulation that the economic structure of capitalist society emerged and liberated from the economic structure of feudal society.
